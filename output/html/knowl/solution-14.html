\(\frac{\fe{h}{3}-\fe{h}{1.5}}{3-1.5}=0\). This value tells us that the average velocity experienced by the ball between the 1.5th second of play and the third second of play is 0 m⁄s. Please note that this does not imply that the ball doesn't move; it simply means that the ball is at the same elevation at the two times.
