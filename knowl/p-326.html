The relevant pattern in the \(y\) column is the powers of \(10\). The first nonzero digit is moving farther and farther to the right of the decimal point. So the \(y\)-values are approaching zero. This suggest that \(\lim\limits_{x\to\infty}\fe{f}{x}=0\). So \(\lim\limits_{x\to\infty}\fe{f}{x}\) exists.
