When using limit laws to establish limit values as \(x\to\infty\) or \(x\to-\infty\), Limit Law A1–Limit Law A6 and Limit Law R2 are still in play (when applied in a valid manner), but Limit Law R1 cannot be applied. (The reason it cannot be applied is discussed in detail in Exercise Group 2.8.1.11–2.8.1.18 from Section 2.8.)
