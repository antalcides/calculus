Solution1.4.1.9.1

\begin{align*} \frac{\fe{s}{t+h}-\fe{s}{t}}{h}&=\frac{\left[(t+h)^3-(t+h)-9\right]-\left[t^3-t-9\right]}{h}\\ &=\frac{t^3+3t^2h+3th^2+h^3-t-h-9-t^3+t+9}{h}\\ &=\frac{3t^2h+3th^2+h^3-h}{h}\\ &=\frac{h(3t^2+3th+h^2-1)}{h}\\ &=3t^2+3th+h^2-1\quad\text{for $h\neq0$} \end{align*}
