While working Exercise Group 1.3.1.18–1.3.1.21 from Activity 1.3 you completed Table 1.3.3. In the context of that problem the difference quotient being evaluated returned the average rate of change in the volume of fluid remaining in a vat between times \(t=4\) and \(t=4+h\). As the elapsed time closes in on \(0\) this average rate of change converges to \(-6\). From that we deduce that the rate of change in the volume \(4\) minutes into the draining process must have been 6 gal⁄min.
