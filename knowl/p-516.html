The acceleration function is:\begin{align*} \fe{a}{t}&=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\fe{v}{t+h}-\fe{v}{t}}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{\left[60-32(t+h)\right]-\left[60-32t\right]}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{60-32t-32h-60+32t}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{-32h}{h}\\ &=\lim_{h\to0}-32\\ &=-32\\ \fe{a}{4.1}&=-32 \end{align*}So 4.1 s into its motion (and in fact at any point during its motion), the object h as acceleration -32 ft⁄s2.
