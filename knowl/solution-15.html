Solution1.4.1.15.1

\(\frac{\fe{P}{29}-\fe{P}{1}}{29-1}=-0.1\). This value tells us that between the first swing and the 29th swing the average rate of change in the pendulum's period is \(-0.1\frac{\text{s}}{\text{swing}}\). In other words, on average, with each passing swing the period decreases by a tenth of a second.
