Solution2.14.1.3.1

You should definitely recognize decimals approaching common fractions. Good catch if you noted the \(t\) was approaching \(\frac{1}{3}\) only from the left. The suggested limit is \(\lim\limits_{t\to\frac{1}{3}^{-}}\fe{z}{t}=\frac{2}{3}\), and this (one-sided) limit exists.
